The False-Negative Fallacy

Client: ID/X Partners — Credit Risk Prediction pipeline built on a highly imbalanced 88:11 Good-to-Bad loan dataset. A model optimized for accuracy is the wrong model. A false negative — approving a bad loan — costs exponentially more than rejecting ten good ones.

XGBoost LightGBM SMOTE StandardScaler Threshold Tuning Python

03
Prep & SMOTE

04
Modelling
Deploy XGBoost / LightGBM
05
Business Tuning
